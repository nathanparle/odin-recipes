Pesto Pizza

Description

Making Pesto Pizza can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics. You probably already have all the ingredients you'll need to make this rich Pesto Pizza. If not, here's what to put on your grocery list:

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • ½ cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • Cocoa-Powder
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Let the Pesto Pizza rest before serving
